The Sanskrit word aaryah (arya) translates as precious and not ordinary. This word inspired the birth of our brand AARYAH, a collection of Indian inspired jewelry.

Think tribal meets simplicity. Made for the confident and trendy risk-taker, the AARYAH woman is fearless and never conventional with her style. Our curated collections are meticulously designed to leave a lasting impression.

Each piece is handmade by skilled Indian artisans ensuring every aspect is crafted to perfection

Megan Kothari was born into a family of jewelers dating back to the early 1900s. As a teenager, she remembers borrowing her mother's handmade silver cuff and wearing it at every occasion possible. Her passion for jewelry was discovered at an early age and her personal style has always been inspired by her Indian heritage.

After attending fashion school in New York City and working in the largest beauty company in the world, it became clear to Megan that it was time to follow her lifelong passion. It was time for her to conquer the world of jewelry!
