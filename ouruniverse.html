Growing up in America, I was influenced by the various forms of art that I found around me. Pop art has always been my favorite because of its loud colors.









India has always been a part of me. I've spent a fourth of my life in Mumbai, exploring new areas each time I am there. The culture and style of India there has really influenced my line, as well as my personal fashion









London is one of my favorite cities. I spent 6 months across the pond when I was in college, and I fell in love with the fast-paced life and unique sense of identity in London.








Egypt was a total culture shock for me. I felt like I was in an alternative universe while I was there. I loved everything from the 1 dollar falfaels to the beautiful embroideries to the roughness of the language.
